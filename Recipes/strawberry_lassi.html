Strawberry Frozen Lassi

Serves 2

Lassi is an important part of indian culture. Especially around summers and on Holi. The festival of colors. Lassi is a good alternative to soda or other carbonated drinks.

It's an affortless, quick and nutrition filled recipe, that you can make with only a blend. Everybody loves icecream. All they need beside an ice-cream is Frozen Lassi with strawberries.

So all the lazy people, It's time to get your ingredients ready and stare directly into the eyes of the sun until it melts abd turns into a slice of mangooye ice-cream.

Ingredients

  • Strawberries
  • Sugar
  • Milk
  • Frozen Yogurt
  • Ice

Steps

  1. Purée Strawberries in a blender or food processor on high. Strain and put 1/2 cup into freezer and reserve the rest for another use.
  2. Reward yourself by relaxing for half an hour but if you're impatient keep reading.
  3. blend sugar, yogurt and the purée until it's even. then add 1/2 teaspoon of lime juice or koshar salt.
  4. Add 1/2 cup pf milk. and put into the freezer or ice-cream churner.
  5. pour or scoop into your glass if everything goes well.
