Chai

Serves 1

What coffee is for the overworked World today, Chai is for indians. It's your lifelong friend, being with you from your morning breakfast to your late night study sessions.

It can be mixed with different ayurvedic ingredients to create a healthy drink or a kadha that helps in cough or cold. Chai has travelled a lot. And with traveling, evolved a lot in flavors and variations. In India alone, there are more than 10 Variations of Chai. And they all are equally delicious

Here's how to make the drink that fuels more than a billion people.

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Ginger
  • Tea powder
  • Cardamom

Steps

  1. Heat 2 cups of water in a vessel or a pan. Wait for the water to become hot, then add 2-3 tablespoons of sugar. You can also add sugar in the end according to you test.
  2. Take a ginger the size of two peas, Crush or grate them then add them to the water.
  3. Let the water simmer for two to three minutes on a medium heat.
  4. crush two cardamom into semi-fine powder and add into the maybe now boiling water.
  5. Let it boil for another two minutes. And think about how bad you're doing in your life.
  6. Assuming that you have bought your favourite brand of tea powder (which i know doesn't look like powder) add them or him or her or it after reading the package because EVERY TEA POWDER IS DIFFERENT. (*panting....*)
  7. Boil more.(It might taste bitter or not but the blog I'm copying this from doesn't say much about it.)
  8. According to your likes add a quarter or half a cup of milk.
  9. Continue to simmer and shine for another one or two minutes. *whispers something boring*
  10. Take it of the heat, strain and then drink in whatever glass you want to. (yes even a wineglass)
